Festival of Belligerents

The Festival of Belligerents is a weekly tournament where the very best artificial brains don their virtual armour and engage in heroic combat for the honour of wearing the Laurel of Steel.

Official Rules

  1. Tournament
    1. There shall be one tournament each week. The tournament shall be conducted before midnight on Sunday.
    2. Each competitor shall enter only one robot.
    3. Each robot shall be packaged using the built-in packager.
    4. Each Robot shall be emailed to the Grand Master of Belligerents by 6PM (California time) on the day of the contest.
      1. If a competitor fails to submit a robot by the appointed time, the most recent version of that robot shall compete.
      2. It shall not be permitted to download or run any other competing robot before the official tournament.
      3. Upon completion of the tournament, all robots shall be available for download for combat practice.
    5. The results of each tournament shall be posted on the official noticeboard along with a recording of the battle.
  2. Battle
    1. Each tournament shall consist of a single battle of 10 rounds.
    2. The battlefield size shall be 1000x1000 where n is the number of competitors..
    3. All robots shall compete together in a single battle.
  3. Robots
    1. No third party code shall be permitted except code from :
      1. robocode.*
      2. java.*
    2. No robot shall reference the name of another robot in its source code.
    3. Source code may not be copied from third party robots.
    4. Reverse engineering of other robots shall not be permitted.
  4. The Winner
    1. The winner shall be the robot which scores the most points according to the built-in Robocode scorer.
  5. Game Settings
    1. The tournament version of Robocode shall be Version 1.9.2.4
    2. The default battle settings shall be used.
    3. Gun cooling rate shall be set to 0.1.
    4. Inactivity time shall be set to 450.
    5. The command line used to start Robocode shall be java -Xms512M -Xmx512M -jar robocode.jar
  6. Violations and Remedies
    1. Minor infringements of these rules shall result in the violator being omitted from that week's tournament.
    2. High crimes and misdemeanors will result in an online poll to determine the continued participation of the violator.
  7. Amendments
    1. Any competitor may propose an amendment to these rules.
    2. Amendments shall be accepted if approved by a majority of competitors in an online poll.
    3. Amended in accordance with all polls to date.
